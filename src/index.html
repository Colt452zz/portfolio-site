About Me

Hi, I'm Colton — a freshman Computer Science student with a passion for building things from the ground up. Growing up playing games sparked a curiosity that eventually became a drive to create them myself, and that passion has shaped the way I approach programming as a whole. I believe the best way to learn is to write real code, break things, and figure out why.

Right now I'm working on a growing portfolio of JavaScript projects, including a clicker game, local multiplayer games like Tic Tac Toe and Chess, and eventually a platformer with AI opponents. My long-term goal is to tackle increasingly complex problems — like implementing unbeatable AI and integrating chess engines — one project at a time.

I'm always open to opportunities that let me keep growing as a developer — whether that's an internship, freelance work, or just an interesting collaboration. Feel free to check out my work on GitHub.
